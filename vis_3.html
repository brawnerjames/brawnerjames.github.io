Cloudiness

There does not appear to be any relationship between the latitude of a city and the cloudiness of that city. This chart plots the average cloud percentage of the set of cities, and there is no correlation between cities with high latitudes or with low latitudes. Cities that sit on the equator appear just as likely to have 100% cloudiness as cities that sit much farther north or south; they are also equally likely to show zero cloudiness.

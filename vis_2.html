Humidity

This analysis does not show a strong relationship between the latitude of a city and its humidity. Overall, the cities surveyed appear to be fairly humid, as well over half of them report a humidity percentage greater than 60%. However, the humid cities are jsut as likely to be of high or low latitudes, and there does not seem to be a general trend.
